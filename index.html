Pharmacogenetics

Powerful New Tools For Personalized Prescribing

Why Clinical Pharmacogenetics?

Plasma drug levels can vary as much as 1000-fold in similar patients taking identical doses of a particular drug.

Variations in drug metabolism are largely determined by a patient's genetic profile.

Make Informed Prescribing Decisions

Predict and prevent adverse drug reactions

Determine the efficacy of a drug for a particular patient

Understand the optimal drug dose

Adverse Drug Reactions (ADRs)

ADRs occur even when the physician writes the correct prescription and the patient takes the medication properly.

Over 100,000 deaths annually in the U.S.

Over 2.2 million illnesses annually in the U.S.

An estimated $389 billion spent annually for ADRs

Advantages

The advantages of the Pharmarisk® swab test allow doctors to reduce adverse drug reactions, minimize liability, maximize treatment success and lower health care costs. Doctors can now personalize patient treatments to their unique profile, giving doctors more power in prescribing drugs correctly and efficiently

Simplicity of Testing

The test is performed on saliva samples. The Pharmarisk® report is available within 5 days of receipt at Affiliated Genetics. The report includes every widely-accepted gene variant implicated in ADRs, and will give you complete knowledge of how a patient will respond to prescriptions.

Fits into Clinical Routine

The Affiliated Genetics Lab handles all insurance billing (Medicare or Private) and provides all shipping materials. The swab tests are shipped to your practice along with return postage. A simple requisition form for each patient will be added to your daily routine. Electronic reporting and easy to interpret results adds ease to the process.

Insurance & Reimbursement

Medicare, HMOs, PPOs, Tricare, VA, and most private insurance companies reimburse for genomic testing if deemed necessary to patient treatment.

A person's genetic makeup does not change so the test only needs to be administered once.

Affiliated Genetics will handle all insurance claims and work to get reimbursement as quickly and efficiently as possible.
